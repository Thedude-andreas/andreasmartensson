SlopeTrace (Android)

Android app for alpine ski sessions with sensor fusion, 3D tracks without map tiles, and event-based stats. Multiple riders can share the same session in real time with Supabase as the backend.

Documentation: Overview

  • Create or join sessions, and explicitly start/stop tracking.
  • Collect GPS, barometer, and accelerometer data with fused altitude and smoothed speed.
  • Automatic segment classification: LIFT, DOWNHILL, and UNKNOWN.
  • 3D rendering of multi-user tracks with rotatable camera and pinch zoom.

Documentation: Key Features

  • Z-gate reduces false-positive classification: LIFT is confirmed after +10 m and DOWNHILL after -10 m (the first 10 m is backfilled on confirmation).
  • Live view refreshes session trails continuously and marks riders as active/inactive.
  • Legend shows live speed and distance to active users in the current session.
  • Background tracking via foreground service.
  • Lift clustering: similar lift traces are grouped into the same physical lift, with editable names.
  • Session and event (run/lift) stats: time, speed, angle, airtime, and vertical drop.
  • Point-history export to JSON when leaving a session for continued analysis/tuning.
  • Account actions include sign up/login, logout in menu, and signed-in alias in header.

Technical Stack

  • Kotlin + Jetpack Compose + OpenGL ES rendering.
  • Room local datastore.
  • Supabase for auth, realtime, and session sync.
